Welcome to Estate Merkez

We are a group four members about to run this startup

Estate Merkez is a real estate database and brokerage service that aims to transform the way people buy, sell and rent properties by offering a combination of powerful online search tools along with a team of in-house agents. EstateMerkez enables users to schedule online appointments and even give or receive offers in a way that makes the whole process more efficient and transparent. EstateMerkez aims to further enhance the user experience by introducing virtual tours, floorplans, videography and a whole lot more.
